6. DATA USAGE FOR POWER OUTAGE PREDICTION
================================================================================

WHY NDVI MATTERS:
  • High NDVI = More trees = Higher risk of tree fall on lines
  • Low NDVI = Less buffer = Higher risk from dust/sand storms
  • NDVI variability = Terrain complexity = Harder maintenance
  • Seasonal NDVI changes = Predict maintenance needs

HOW TO USE:
  1. Merge with hourly weather data (forward-fill NDVI values)
  2. Create interaction features (NDVI × rainfall, NDVI × wind)
  3. Calculate NDVI change rates (vegetation growth speed)
  4. Flag peak vegetation periods (highest risk times)

EXPECTED FEATURES IN MODEL:
  • ndvi_mean - Base vegetation level
  • ndvi_max - Peak vegetation (highest risk)
  • ndvi_change_rate - Growth/decline speed
  • is_high_vegetation - Binary flag (NDVI > 0.7)
  • ndvi_seasonal_deviation - Abnormal vegetation
